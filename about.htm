I'm a 3D Animation student at Saint Paul College! In addition to my growing expertise in that field, I have experience in music production and sound design as a result of my own personal projects and those I've helped others with. On this page is a compendium of what I consider to be my standard of work, but it is by no means an exhaustive list. Contact me for more information!

Music

I've enjoyed good music for as long as I can remember, but my first go at the world of arrangement came with creating custom MIDI soundfonts out of various noises. Frustrated by the limits of copyright, I began to write my own music. While I've stepped into other genres, my typical style is fast-paced, EDM-inspired orchestral-fusion tracks, reminiscent of videogames.



Effects

The first time I set out to record sound effects with a purpose was for a vintage-themed collaborative radio show. The project fell through, but I recorded a number of effects for it. At the time I only had a monophonic dynamic microphone, but I have continued to upgrade my equipment and still record new sounds to date.
